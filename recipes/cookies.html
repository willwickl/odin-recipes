Cookies

Description

The thickest chocolate chip cookies ever made topped with cane sugar.

Ingredients:

  • 3 1/4 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 cup of sweet cream butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 3/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 tsp of pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 12 oz of chocolate chips
  • 1 secret ingredient

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, salt, and secret ingredient
  3. Beat butter, sugars, and vanilla extract in large mixer bowl until creamy
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition
  5. Gradually beat in flour mixture
  6. Stir in chocolate chips
  7. Place rounded dough on baking sheets
  8. Bake for 10-14 minutes
  9. Cool for 2 minutes before moving to wire racks to cool completely
