Brownies

Description

World's best pan of brownies. Best if in a bigger pan.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of butter
  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 tablespoon and 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2-1 cup of chocolate chips (size doesn't matter)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes give or take. Check with toothpick.
  4. Remove from oven and let cool for at least 15 minutes.
