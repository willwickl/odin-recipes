Chocolate Toffee Cookies

Description

Award winning chocolate toffee cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 1 pound of bittersweet or semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 stick of butter (4 tbs) unsalted butter
  • 1 and 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tbs of vanilla extract
  • 7 oz of Heath Bits (or other toffee)
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts, toasted
  • (OPTIONAL) Sea Salt for sprinkling

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  2. In a double boiler over gently simmering water (or in a bowl in a microwave), combine the chocolate and butter, stirring occasionally, until melted and smooth. Remove from heat and cool until the mixture is lukewarm.
  3. In mixer on medium speed, beat the brown sugar and eggs until thick, about 5 minutes.
  4. Reduce speed to low. Add the chocolate mixture from step 2 and vanilla extract and mix until well combined
  5. Add the flour mixture and mix until just combined. Stir in toffee walnuts.
  6. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  7. When ready to bake, preheat the oven to 350F and line the baking sheet with parchment paper.
  8. Drop heaping tablespoons of the dough two inches apart on the sheet. Sprinkle each cookie with sea salt if desired.
  9. Bake until the tops of cookies are dry and cracked but are still soft to the touch, about 12-14 minutes; do not overbake.
  10. Let cookies cool for 2 minutes after removing from the oven. Then transfer to wire rack to cool completely.
